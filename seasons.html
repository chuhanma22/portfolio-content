Seasons

Season of Gratitude, beginning July 19th, 2019 and ending September 2nd, 2019, was the first season of the full game. It was mostly a repeat of the beta version of the same event, with minor changes.

The Season of Lightseekers began on September 23, 2019, and ended on November 10, 2019.

The Season of Belonging began on November 18, 2019, and ended on January 12, 2020. It centered around a family of six spirits trying to find their way home.

The Season of Rhythm began Friday, January 24, 2020, and ended on Sunday, April 5, 2020. This season brought revamped Spirits and Cosmetics, as well as some new collectibles. Home was given a snowy makeover with an ice rink, and Traveling Spirits were introduced to the game.

The Season of Enchantment began on Monday, April 20, 2020, and ended on the Sunday, June 21, 2020. This season introduced a new area, in which all of the season exclusive content and quests could be found. Adding a new area became standard for all seasons after this. It also added a shop where players can purchase spells.ÃŸ

The Season of Sanctuary began Monday, July 13, 2020, and ended on Monday, September 21, 2020. This summer-themed vacation season introduced another new seasonal area. The start of the season was also marked by Sky's One Year Anniversary celebrations.

The Season of Prophecy began on Monday, October 5, 2020, and ended on Sunday, December 13, 2020. It introduced the Cave of Prophecies area. It also added new challenges called "trials".

The Season of Dreams began on January 4, 2021, and ended on March 15th 2021. This season introduced two new areas, and followed the journey of a young skater who dreams of performing in the Coliseum. It also added new races, as well as making improvements to previous game areas.

The Season of Assembly began on April 5, 2021, and ended on June 13, 2021. It added a feature in which players can decorate their own rooms.[11] The season followed a group of children as they seek to build a treehouse together.

The Season of The Little Prince' began on July 6, 2021, and ended on September 19th, 2021. The Season of the Little Prince was a re-telling of the classic novella, The Little Prince, in which players can interact with the book's characters, in celebration of its 75th anniversary. It is notable in being Skys first collaborative season.[12] In it, the players guide a royal visitor through the realms of Sky to rediscover important connections.

The Season of Flight began on October 4, 2021, and ended on December 19th, 2021. It adds a new area to the game world.

The Season of Abyss began on January 17, 2022, and ended on March 27th, 2022. It was the first season to introduce a Diving and Swimming mechanism, and also the first to make an underwater location for players to explore.

The Season of Performance began on April 11, 2022, and ended on June 26th, 2022. The Season of Performance added a stage location where players can customize background, lighting, and effects. It also added a brand-new feature called "shared memories" where players can make a video-like message for other players to watch.

The Season of Shattering began on July 11th, 2022 and ended on September 25th, 2022. This season adopts a new format, taking place in almost all realms.
